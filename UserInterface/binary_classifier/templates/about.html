Our Project

Spring 2021 Senior Capstone Design Project

Motivations

Amazon is the world’s largest online marketplace and one of the world's most valuable companies. There are an immense number of products sold on Amazon and they have a correspondingly immense amount of images Being able better understand these images benefits the company and its end users.

Design Problem

There are many products in Amazon’s catalog that look very similar but contain small differences that can be found upon close inspection. However, it is a tedious task to manually detect these differences with the human eye. Given two images, can a model determine if they represent the same product?

This is where our project “Understanding Product Images” comes in. We are team H19 and our goal is to finetune and train a deep neural net based ML model that determines if two images are of the same product and creates a bounding box highlighting the difference if they are not.
